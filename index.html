A Young Scientist 2023 project using an Arduino, PIR sensor, and buzzer to alert visually impaired users to oncoming vehicles through infrared movement detection and a wrist buzz.

Memory metal crumple zones

A Young Scientist project investigating nitinol as a material for car crumple zones, with the aim of making repairs safer, cheaper, and less wasteful.

Spark Innovation

I am currently assistant manager of Spark Innovation. We run STEM clubs in Headford and Galway, hold regular meetings, and bring in speakers so students have more support if they want to build, compete, or explore STEM seriously.

I manage Spark's social media, work on outreach and event management, and do a lot of cold emailing to help the organisation grow.
